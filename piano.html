I've been playing piano for over 10 years. I started my career as a kid, and as I expanded my skills, I competed in competitions and regularly took exams. After winning second place in Crescendo International Music Competition in 2020, I got the chance to perform at Carnegie Hall in New York City in 2022. Later that year, I participated in the competition again, winning first place. I got the opportunity to perform at Carnegie Hall for the second time in 2023.
